I am Zheyuan Zhang, born in Lanzhou, Gansu province, China. I am a final-year PhD student at Dyson School of Design Engineering, Imperial College London. I graduated from Tongji University in 2017 and got my postgraduate degree in Innovation Design Engineering (IDE) at the Royal College of Art and Imperial College London in 2019.
